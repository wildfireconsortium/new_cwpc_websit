Our Scorecard Partnership

Catastrophic Wildfire Prevention Consortium (CWPC) at CrowdDoing

CWPC is a cross-sector, international consortium of wildfire experts, institutions and volunteers focused on preventing catastrophic wildfires through a portfolio of customized, synergistic innovations for communities.

CWPC is establishing the collective opportunity for net risk reduction by communities, achieved through multi-stakeholder collaboration using costs and liabilities of inaction model to finance action to prevent wildfire risks, and with a synergistic portfolio of new and traditional strategies for prevention, mitigation and adaptation.

CrowdDoing

CrowdDoing (CD) is a joint initiative of the Match4Action Foundation and ReframeIt, focused on addressing the social, economic, and environmental challenges our world faces by collaborating with professionals and volunteers from many different industries. CD aims to support social innovations with transformative impact potential through global multi-disciplinary volunteering, micro-leadership and service learning.

Learn more about CD Catastrophic Wildfire Prevention Consortium

ARISE-US

ARISE-US is the US network of ARISE, established by the United Nations (UN) to build public-private collaboration in disaster resilience. We are a 100% volunteer organization operating in 35 countries worldwide, with 450 corporate members and many more NGO and governmental partners. We published the original UN City Disaster Resilience Scorecard, now used by roughly 350 cities worldwide (including many in the US), on which this wildfire-specific scorecard is modeled and several other scorecards, and a free critical asset management tool.
